Testing

Software testing is an investigation provided by All Your Bugs of your product and services that can be used to provide status updates to Stakeholders. Software testing can also provide an objective, independent view of the software to allow the business to appreciate and understand the risks of software implementation. It can also be used to determine where defects and bugs in the software tested.

Web Design

Web design encompasses many different skills and disciplines in the production and maintenance of websites. The different areas of web design provided by All Your Bugs include web graphic design; interface design; authoring, including standardised code and proprietary software; user experience design; and search engine optimization.

Development

All Your Bugs provides Software development. Our group can write and maintain the source code, and involve ourselves in the conception of the desired software through to the final manifestation of the software, in a planned and structured process. Our group can supply our expertise in include research, new development, prototyping, modification, reuse, re-engineering, and maintenance.

Project Management

Project management provided All Your Bugs is the process and activity of planning, organizing, motivating, and controlling resources to achieve specific goals. Our group can assist your company with achieving all of your project goals and objectives while honoring your preconceived constraints.
